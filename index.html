Places I Visited

Here are some interesting facts about the places I traveled to over the years:

Manali

A beautiful hill station nestled in the Himalayas, famous for its stunning landscapes and adventure sports.

Delhi

The bustling capital city of India, rich in history, culture, and delicious street food.

Pondicherry

A charming coastal town known for its French colonial architecture and serene beaches.

Ayodhya

An ancient city with deep religious significance, believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama.

Ujjain

One of the four sites of the Kumbh Mela, known for its ancient temples and spiritual atmosphere.

Coorg

A picturesque hill station famous for coffee plantations, waterfalls, and lush greenery.

Mysore

Known for its royal heritage, majestic palaces, and the famous Dasara festival.
